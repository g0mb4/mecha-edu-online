2nd homework: Shear and bending moment diagrams

Statics 2nd homework

The structural beam illustrated in the program is subjected to a concentrated force, a uniformly distributed load, and a concentrated moment.
Determine the system of support reactions through analytical calculation.
Based on the calculated values, construct the shear and bending moment diagrams for the beam.
Utilizing these diagrams, determine by calculation:
  • the magnitude of the maximum shear force;
  • the magnitude of the maximum bending moment;
  • and their respective distances from the origin of the coordinate system.
