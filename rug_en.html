Theory of elasticity 1st homework

The statically indeterminate frame illustrated by the program is subjected to a concentrated force, a concentrated moment, and a uniformly distributed load.
Determine the reaction forces/moments and construct the shear and bending moment diagrams for the frame.
Calculate the magnitude of the maximum bending moment.
The frame features a constant cross-section and is fabricated from an I-beam profile (MSZ 325-77, superseded by MSZ EN 10024:1999), with external loads acting in the direction of the profile's y-axis.
The dimensions of the frame's cross-section are provided by the software.
Calculate the magnitude of the maximum normal stress.
